Metal Bands and Metal culture

Metal Culture

The metal scene usually is known for aggressive, hard music with rough vocals.

Yet still, there are some Metal bands almost everyone knows!

Here are some examples:

  • Metallica
  • Iron Maiden
  • Rob Zombie
  • Slayer
  • Led Zeppelin

These Metal Bands are highly successful, but it is totally understandable if not every Band named above are your type.

Since there are many different types of metal, it is very likely that there is a genre of metal you simply didn't know of

Fun Facts about Metal
  • Dave Mustaine of Megadeth chose heavy metal as a career as revenge against a brother-in-law who punched him for listening to Judas Priest
  • You cannot find lyrics of Gorgoroth's songs anywhere.
  • Before settling on Metallica, one of the names Lars suggested for his band was Thunderfuck.
  • The lead singer of Ghost maintained strict anonymity ever since the bands creation.
